Pico de Gallo

Description

The freshness of homemade pico de gallo tops anything store bought. And with the simplicity of making it I wont be going back!

Eat pico de gallo with tortilla chips, or add to your favorite taco or burrito recipe for a kick of acidity and freshness. On another note, I recently realized how similar the ingredients are for ceviche, but that's a recipe for another time.

Ingredients

  • 4 roma tomatoes
  • 2/3 cup white onion (about one small onion)
  • 1 cilantro bunch
  • 1 lime
  • 1 jalapeno or serrano pepper (serranos are spicier than jalapenos, omit peppers if you're not in to spice.)

Steps

  1. Finely chop tomatoes, onions, jalapeno or serrano, and cilantro. Place in a bowl.
  2. Cut lime in half and squeeze each half over your mix.
  3. Season with salt to taste. I like to season, stir, season again, and taste until the salt levels are right.
  4. Let the mixture marinate in your fridge for 15 minutes or longer for the flavors to meld. Or eat immediately if you can't wait!

Notes

Adjust the different amounts of each ingredient to your liking. I often just eyeball how much I want to use.
